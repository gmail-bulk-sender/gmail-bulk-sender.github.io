Privacy Policy for officeworkhelp.in

Last updated: January 2025

At officeworkhelp.in, your privacy and data protection are extremely important. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our web-based tools, such as the Bulk Email Sender or Incident Report Formatter, we may collect:

  • Google Account profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Incident report content you submit voluntarily
  • Uploaded photos (for incident reporting purposes only)
  • Authentication metadata (Google ID, login time)

No unnecessary or sensitive personal information is collected without user consent.

2. How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used only for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate users securely using Google Sign-In
  • To generate formal incident reports for workplace documentation
  • To allow the administrator (sche004@gmail.com) to review and manage submitted reports
  • To process your email sending tasks (Bulk Email tool only)

3. Google OAuth and API Use

officeworkhelp.in uses Google APIs for authentication (OAuth) and optional email sending. We follow the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Incident Report Tool

Only these non-sensitive scopes are used: 

openid
https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.email
https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.profile

These scopes allow identity verification only. We do not access emails, Drive files, or any private Google data.

Bulk Email Tool

If you use the Bulk Email sender, these optional scopes may be requested: 

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send
https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.readonly

We NEVER store or share email contents, attachments, or Drive data.
